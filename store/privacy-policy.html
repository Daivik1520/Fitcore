FitCore Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

FitCore is a 100% offline app. No data ever leaves your device. Period.

What Data Does FitCore Store?

FitCore stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your name, age, weight, and height (entered during setup)
  • Step count history (from your device's pedometer sensor)
  • Workout logs, exercise records, and personal bests
  • Body measurements and weight log
  • Progress photos (stored in app's local storage)
  • Water intake and daily check-in data
  • App preferences and settings

Data Collection

FitCore does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app:

  • Does NOT connect to any server or API
  • Does NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • Does NOT contain advertisements
  • Does NOT require an account or login
  • Does NOT access your contacts, location, or any data beyond motion sensors

Permissions

FitCore requests the following device permissions:

  • Activity Recognition / Motion Sensors: Used solely to count your steps via the device pedometer. This data is stored locally and never transmitted.
  • Camera (optional): Only used if you choose to take progress photos. Photos are saved to the app's private storage on your device.
  • Notifications (optional): Used to send local workout reminders and step goal alerts. No data is sent to any server.

Data Storage

All data is stored using your device's local storage (AsyncStorage). Data persists between app sessions but is permanently deleted if you:

  • Use the "Reset All Data" option in Settings
  • Uninstall the app
  • Clear the app's storage in device settings

You can export your data as a CSV or JSON backup file at any time from the Profile screen.

Third-Party Services

FitCore uses zero third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms. The app functions entirely offline with no network requests.

Children's Privacy

FitCore does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app stores all information locally on the device and does not transmit any data.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Since FitCore is offline, no in-app notification is needed.
