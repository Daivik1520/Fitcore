## App Name

## Short Description (80 chars max)

## Full Description

FitCore is your complete fitness companion that works entirely offline. No account. No internet. No excuses.

STEP TRACKING
Track your daily steps with an animated progress ring. Set personal goals from 4,000 to 15,000 steps and watch your ring fill up throughout the day. See your weekly step history in beautiful bar charts.

GUIDED WORKOUT PROGRAMS
Choose from 5 complete programs designed for every fitness level:
- Fat Loss — 12-week cardio + strength program
- Muscle Gain — Push/pull/legs split for building strength
- Flexibility — 30-day mobility challenge
- Stamina — HIIT interval training progression
- Beginner — Start from zero with guided progression

40+ BODYWEIGHT EXERCISES
Every exercise includes step-by-step instructions, common mistakes to avoid, and animated demonstrations. No gym needed — train anywhere with just your body.

PROGRESSIVE OVERLOAD
FitCore automatically tracks your performance and suggests when to increase difficulty. See your rep progression over time with detailed charts.

SMART REST TIMER
Rest periods auto-adjust based on exercise difficulty. Recovery zone indicators help you rest the right amount.

COMPLETE PROGRESS TRACKING
- Body weight & measurements tracker
- Workout streak calendar
- Muscle group heat map
- Exercise personal records
- Weekly comparison reports
- Before & after progress photos

DAILY WELLNESS
- Water intake tracker (8-glass goal)
- Daily mood & energy check-in
- Motivational quotes
- Focus timer for meditation & stretching

ACHIEVEMENT SYSTEM
Earn 20 badges for milestones like first workout, streak records, step goals, and personal bests. Unlock premium app themes by building workout streaks.

CUSTOM WORKOUTS
Build your own workout by picking from the full exercise library. Add personal notes to exercises to track what works for you.

YOUR DATA, YOUR DEVICE
FitCore stores everything locally on your phone. Export your data as CSV or create full JSON backups anytime. No server. No tracking. No ads. Just results.

WARM-UP & COOL-DOWN
Every workout automatically includes a guided warm-up and cool-down routine to prevent injury and improve recovery.

Download FitCore and start your fitness journey today. It takes 25 minutes. No equipment. No excuses.
